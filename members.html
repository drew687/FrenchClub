Members

The students of French club are people who want to learn more about French culture than what they already learn in their classes. They enjoy their time with other people who have the same interests as they do. The members like taking part in French culture with friends that want to have fun also. Everyone in the club likes to come together and participate in fun activites. The club has many people that are accepting and who are open to new friendships.
